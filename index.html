The Inspiration

Sheikh Farid Ji
The Great Sufi Saint of India

A 12th-century Sufi saint, Sheikh Farid is revered as the great Sufi saint of India — the ancient voice whose cry of longing became the very breath of this retreat.

Beliefs and Impact

Baba Farid Ji, made the path of the heart accessible to everyone by sharing his wisdom in the everyday language of the people. He fiercely fought against empty rituals and hypocrisy, teaching that the Divine resides within the human heart rather than in wilderness penances.

His life embodies the path of Universal Love, illustrating that true spirituality transcends labels and is an internal journey.

Rooted in Forgiveness, he showed that compassion is the strongest response to hostility. Through the power of "Birha", a deep, purifying yearning for the Beloved, combined with Patience and Simplicity, he demonstrated that a quiet, devoted heart is the true way to the Divine.

Listen to the Wise
Bhai Bahadur Singh Ji
Known to many as Bhaji or Bhai Sahib Ji, he is the humble servant of Guru Nanak — walking with devotion in the footsteps of his spiritual Master, Baba Jaswant Singh Ji, known lovingly as Babaji.

Bhai Sahib Ji is a teacher of Divine values that transcend caste, gender, social and religious backgrounds — calling for the praise of the One Divine in all, and the service of the One through a clean, innocent heart, like a child. To be in his presence during this journey is a rare and treasured gift.
